Travel Safety Tips

  • Keep your valuables secure and avoid showing expensive items in public.
  • Always stay aware of your surroundings and avoid isolated places at night.
  • Carry a basic first aid kit and drink plenty of water.
  • Make digital and physical copies of important documents like your passport.
  • Share your travel plans with a family member or friend.
  • Know local emergency contact numbers and your country's embassy information.
  • Use trusted hotels and official transportation services.
